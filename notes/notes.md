# Problem Solving Patterns Cheat Sheet

One of the most important skills in **Problem Solving** (especially for LeetCode and Technical Interviews) is recognizing the **pattern** of a problem quickly.

When you read the problem and detect the pattern early, you can immediately start thinking about the right approach.

Over time you will notice that certain **keywords** in the problem strongly indicate which algorithm or technique you should use.
